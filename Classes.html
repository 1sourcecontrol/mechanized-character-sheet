Pilot Classes

Soldier of Fortune

The Soldier of fortune takes bounties on the players that they kill. They get a credit bonus every time they make the attack that blows up an enemey. How much they get depends on how many players are in the game.

Players Credits

Veteran Soldier

The Veteran Soldier is more used to using their weapons in the heat of combat. As such, they are slightly more accurate with them. They get a +1 accuracy to one of the following weapon categories.

  • Tier 1 weapons
  • Tier 2 weapons
  • Tier 3 weapons
  • Lasers & Polaron Cannon
  • Plasma, Particle, and Ion weapons
  • Gauss & Rail weapons
  • Autocannon, Rotary, and Chain, guns
  • Missiles
  • Melee weapons

Mech Engineer

The Mech Engineer is tallented at reducing the unnecessary resistance in their Mech's systems, and can squeeze a little more energy out of their reactors every round.

Mech Mechanic

The Mechanic has the tools and skills to machine precision parts that perform better. Their mech gets an additional +5 ammo storage. If a mech mechanic gets shields, they get +5 strength.

Scavenger

Can be used once per battle. If a player has fallen he has to tell you what weapons he is using (if you ask), you can purchase one of the weapons he is using from the market at the end of the match with an (up to) 15 credit bonus. You must survive the battle to do the latter.

Banker

Receive 20% interest on previously saved money (round up) at the start of every round. Any money cards used have +12 credits after round 1.

Hacker

The hacker gets a bonus for selling stolen IDs from opponents hacked during the fighting. The hacker gets extra credits for each unique player hacked each round. How much they get per successful hack depends on how many players are playing. Use a hacking subsystem to do hacking.
