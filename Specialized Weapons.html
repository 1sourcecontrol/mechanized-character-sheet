Requires the Heavy Weapons: Plasma Chaingun specialization card in order to buy.

Deals double damage to shields. If the shields are exhausted, the remainder of the damage is dealt to the chassis of the target without the 2x multiplier. For example, if a target has 3 shields left and this weapon would deal 3 damage, then this weapon would pop the shields, and then do 1 damage to the target's health.
